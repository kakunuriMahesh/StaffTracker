Privacy Policy

StaffTracker Application

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

We at StaffTracker ("we", "our", or "us") are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Important: StaffTracker is designed with privacy at its core. Your data never leaves your device.

2. Information We Collect

📱

Local Device Storage

All data (staff profiles, attendance, salary info) is stored ONLY on your device using local SQLite database.

🔧

App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app performance and user experience.

📶

Device Information

Basic device information (OS version, app version) may be collected for compatibility purposes.

3. What We DON'T Collect

We want to be completely transparent. We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your personal identification information
  • ❌ Staff member personal data
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or calendar information
  • ❌ Financial or banking information
  • ❌ Any data transmitted to external servers

4. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal data, here's what we do with the information stored locally:

  • Store and manage your staff records locally
  • Calculate salaries and generate payslips
  • Track attendance records
  • Enable data export/sharing features you request

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • When you explicitly request to share a payslip with someone
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety

6. Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data storage
  • No cloud sync or external data transmission
  • You control who has access to your device

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all data
  • You can delete individual staff records within the app
  • No automatic data backup to cloud services

8. Third-Party Services

StaffTracker may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

9. Children's Privacy

StaffTracker is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the app
  • Delete any or all data at any time
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Opt-out of any optional data collection

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@stafftracker.app
  • Website: www.stafftracker.app

Summary: StaffTracker is built with your privacy in mind. We don't collect your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We believe in simple, transparent privacy practices.
