Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Staff Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Staff Data: Employee names, positions, salaries, attendance records, and advance payments
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your staff records and attendance
  • To calculate and track salary payments
  • To sync data across your devices via Google Drive
  • To provide customer support

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device
  • Optionally in your personal Google Drive (if sync is enabled)

We do not maintain our own servers. Your data remains under your control.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to outside parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Google (for authentication and optional cloud sync)
  • You - as an exportable option

6. Subscription & Payments

For premium plans:

  • Payments are processed securely via Google Play Store
  • We do not store credit card information
  • Subscription renews automatically unless cancelled

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes via the app.
